The original price of $1,195,000 positioned your home in a competitive bracket with slower absorption. At $1,150,000, we're now positioned as the best value in the Ventura midtown market — signaling seller motivation and removing psychological barriers for buyers who felt the original price was slightly high.

This ~3.8% reduction is strategic enough to trigger automated buyer alerts and re-engage prospects who previously passed.

Buyer Psychology at Work

The "Fresh Listing" Effect

Price reductions trigger MLS notifications to buyers who've saved searches in this price range. Your home appears as "new activity" in their inbox — even if they've seen it before.

The "Deal" Perception

Buyers who previously saw the home at $1,195,000 now perceive $45,000 in additional value. This creates urgency: "If I don't move now, someone else will."

The "Competitive" Mindset

At $1,150,000, we're now competing attractively in the market. Your Channel Islands views, paid-off solar, and ADU potential now stand out even more.
